Data Analytics
1. What is Data Analytics?

Data Analytics is the process of collecting, cleaning, transforming, and analyzing data to discover meaningful insights that help organizations make better business decisions.

Data Analysts use various tools and techniques to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities from data.

2. Where is Data Analytics Used?

Data Analytics is used in many industries, including:

E-Commerce
Banking and Finance
Healthcare
Education
Telecommunications
Marketing
Retail
Manufacturing

Examples:

Analyzing customer purchasing behavior.
Tracking sales performance.
Monitoring business growth.
Predicting future trends.
